ABOUT US

Pizza place is a locally owned pizza restaurant that has been in business for over seven years since it's establishment in early 2013.

We have tried our best to create a family friendly dining experience for our customers and develop deep roots in the community this is where we went ahead and included home delivery for all pizza orders when our attention was raised that pizza is a family meal.

Our major aim at PIZZA place is to be friendly familiar place where you will always be confortable.

The pizza business is full of large national chains, but pizza place loyal customers have made us the busiest and most popular pizza restaurants around. We thank you and will continue to work hard to deserve your business.
